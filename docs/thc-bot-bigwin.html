Internal Document

THC Bot now monitors the community wins channel in real time. When a member posts a screenshot, the bot uses AI to analyse the image and determine whether it qualifies as a big win. Qualifying posts are automatically highlighted with a reaction and reposted into the dedicated big wins channel, ensuring standout moments never go unnoticed.

Detection Rules
01
Highest Priority

PayPal Payouts from THC Circle LLC

Any PayPal payment screenshot showing "THC Circle LLC" as the sender is automatically classified as a big win, regardless of the dollar amount. A $100 payout qualifies the same as a $10,000 payout. This rule takes precedence over all others.

02
GMV Dashboards

Creator Analytics and GMV Screenshots

Screenshots showing a GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) figure from a creator dashboard or analytics screen qualify as a big win only when the GMV displayed is $20,000 USD or more. GMV figures below this threshold do not qualify.

03
General Earnings

All Other Payout and Earnings Screenshots

For any other type of earnings, commission, or payout screenshot, the bot requires a clearly visible dollar amount exceeding $1,000 USD in order to classify the post as a big win.

How It Works
📸

Member Posts

A member shares a screenshot in the wins channel

🤖

AI Reviews

The bot sends the image to GPT-4o for classification

🔥

Reaction Added

Qualifying posts receive a fire reaction instantly

📢

Reposted

The win is shared in the big wins channel as an embed

Image analysis is performed via the OpenAI API. No member data or images are stored externally. A per-user cooldown is in place to prevent duplicate classifications within a short time window.
